[FlashVTG](https://github.com/Zhuo-Cao/FlashVTG) implementation. FlashVTG in
turn incorporates components from DAB-DETR, Conditional DETR, DETR, PyTorch,
and CG-DETR. The original copyright and license headers are retained in the
copied source files where supplied upstream.

Notable components include:

- `blocks/`: FlashVTG components carrying the upstream BSD 3-Clause notice.

  Conditional DETR, DETR, and PyTorch, with their upstream Apache and project
  notices retained.
- the remaining FlashVTG model, training, and evaluation code: adapted from
  the official FlashVTG repository for Generalized Moment Retrieval.
